# Insurance Products

## Supported Insurance Types

- **Motor insurance** — Car, motorcycle, commercial vehicles
- **Home insurance** — Property, contents, landlord
- **Travel insurance** — Single trip, annual multi-trip
- **Life insurance** — Term life, whole life, critical illness
- **Specialty insurance** — See specialty_insurance.md for marine, yacht, high-value assets

## General Coverage Areas

Standard products cover typical risks. For specialty or high-value requests, escalation to the specialty insurance team may be required.
