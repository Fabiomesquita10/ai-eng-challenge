# Specialty Insurance

## Overview

Specialty insurance covers high-value assets and non-standard risks that require dedicated underwriting.

## Supported Specialty Products

- **Marine insurance** — Boats, yachts, watercraft
- **Yacht insurance** — Pleasure craft, sailing yachts, motor yachts
- **High-value asset insurance** — Art, jewellery, collectibles
- **Commercial specialty** — Aviation, marine cargo, professional indemnity

## Yacht Insurance

Yacht insurance requests are handled by the **Specialty Insurance Support Team**. Coverage includes:

- Hull and machinery
- Third-party liability
- Personal effects
- Crew coverage (where applicable)

## Routing

Requests involving "yacht", "marine", "boat", "high-value assets" should be routed to the specialty insurance team. Premium customers receive priority handling.

## Escalation

For values above a certain threshold, cases are escalated to the senior underwriters. Premium customers have dedicated relationship managers.
