# Premium Insurance Routing

## Premium Customer Handling

Premium customers receive priority routing for insurance requests.

## Teams Responsible

- **Standard insurance** — Standard support team
- **Specialty insurance** — Specialty Insurance Support Team (marine, yacht, high-value)
- **Premium escalation** — Premium Relationship Team for high-value or complex cases

## Routing Policies

1. Premium + yacht/marine/high-value → Specialty Insurance Support Team
2. Premium + standard product → Standard team with priority flag
3. High-value claims → Escalation to senior underwriters

## Escalation Paths

- Standard: insurance.support@bank.com
- Specialty: specialty.insurance@bank.com
- Premium: premium.insurance@bank.com
